# v1 Targets Solid Basic-to-Medium Compatibility

Hodei Diagrams v1.0 will target solid basic-to-medium `.drawio` compatibility rather than broad high-fidelity coverage of every complex stencil, asset, and edge case from the start. The decision is to make a useful, predictable, and testable subset truly reliable first, focusing on common nodes, text, edges, groups, pages, common styles, and stable round-trip behavior before expanding into the long tail.
